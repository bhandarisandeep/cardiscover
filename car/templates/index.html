About Us

BlackGarage is an auto portal based out of India. Car Finder operates as a platform for business-to-consumer (B2C) operations. BlackGarage provides lead generation services for new cars. It also provides reviews, price information, on-road prices and comparison facilities for new cars, as well as a forum for the customers to communicate with experts. Car Finder has an inbuilt emi calculator for ease.
We're proud of who we are and where we've come. We are independent of the auto industry's advertising rupee and intend to keep things that way. BlackGarage is committed to its sole purpose of empowering the Indian car owner & enthusiast.
